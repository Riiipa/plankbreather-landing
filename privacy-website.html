Website Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 29, 2025

About This Website

This privacy policy applies to the Plank Breather website (plankbreather.com). For information about our mobile app's privacy practices, please see our App Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This includes:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • How you found our website (search engines, social media, etc.)
  • Your device type, browser, and approximate location (country/city level)
  • Interactions with website elements (button clicks, scroll depth)

Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your consent preferences
  • Analyze website usage via Google Analytics
  • Improve our website performance

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve our website design and user experience
  • Understand which content is most valuable to visitors
  • Monitor website performance and fix technical issues
  • Make data-driven decisions about our marketing efforts

Data Sharing

We share data only with Google Analytics for website analysis purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Google Analytics may use this data according to their own privacy policy. You can learn more about Google's practices at Google Privacy Policy.

Your Rights and Choices

Cookie Consent

You can choose to accept or decline cookies through our consent banner. If you decline, we will not collect analytics data about your visit.

Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings or opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect information transmitted to our analytics providers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at plankbreather@gmail.com.
